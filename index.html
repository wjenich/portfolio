WHAT I DO

UX

I work from the ground up and start everything with a pencil. I research, understanding my costumer's strengths, weaknesses opportunities and competition. I make site maps, user stories and wireframes to make sure the structure of the site I'm working on is sound. And most importantly, I test with users fix things and test again.

UI

With a BA in Graphic Design and Advertising, design comes second nature to me. I make wireframes come alive with vivid color, imagry and typography. Good design helps effectively communicate with the user and that is what I intend with each detail and decision I make. It's my passion to make seamlessly beautiful and intuitive digital products.

Frontend

I like to take a project from start to finish, which is why I also am proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and JQuery.
