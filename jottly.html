Jott.ly

Overview

Jott.ly is a collaborative writing site dedicated to helping people share ideas and thoughts. My task was to design a logo and landing page that explained and encouraged people to sign up for their service.

I drew a number of rapid wireframes to help me choose the best layout of information for the project. I also sketched out many different options for a logo, widdling it down to 10, and then one, respectively. I built the final logo option in Adobe Illustrator. My final product was made in Sketch.
