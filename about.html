ABOUT

Hi, I’m Whitney. I am a UX / UI designer in the Omaha metropolitan area. I have a passion for pixel perfect design and building something from the ground up. I also have skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and JQuery.

Besides learning about my process, I would love to tell you more about myself. I come from a family of on-the-lip-kissers where Seinfeld is religion and one of my mother’s hobbies includes collecting complimentary toiletries from hotels. I see the barista’s at my favorite coffeeshop more than I see my best friends and if I’m not there, you can find me in a pool, on a run, or jamming out at a concert. I’ve also got mean dancing skills that I've been told resemble those of Elaine Benes, I'm inclined to take it as a compliment. Above all, I’m a creative, passionate and goal-driven individual who will take any opportunity to stretch my brain.
